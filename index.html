ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Jonathan, and I've been coding for less than a year, but I plan on continuing to learn more and advancing myself on the path to become a great web developer!

I got into coding from friends already in it, and I felt that there were relatable parallels to architecture, which I started out in. I'm intruiged by the idea of designing an aesthetically pleasing space while also making it functional behind the scenes, though it is now on the web rather than as a building. Being from the field of architecture, I'm quite used to working with clients, supervisors, and team members on projects, but with coding there seems to be much less stress without having to also constantly communicate and coordinate between various city agencies, inspectors, vendors, and contractors.
